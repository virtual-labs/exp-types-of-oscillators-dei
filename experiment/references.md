### Textbooks

1. OpenStax. (2016). *University Physics Volume 1*. OpenStax CNX.  
   https://openstax.org/details/books/university-physics-volume-1  
   (Chapter 15: Simple Harmonic Motion, Energy in SHM, Pendulums, Damped Oscillations, Forced Oscillations.)

2. Taylor, J. R. (2005). *Classical Mechanics*. University Science Books.  
   (Chapter 10: Oscillations; Chapter 11: Coupled Oscillators and Normal Modes.)

3. French, A. P. (1971). *Vibrations and Waves*. M.I.T. Introductory Physics Series. W.W. Norton & Company.  
   (Republished by CRC Press/Taylor & Francis, 2003. Comprehensive coverage of SHM, damped and forced oscillations.)

4. Pain, H. J. (2005). *The Physics of Vibrations and Waves* (6th ed.). Wiley.

5. Main, I. G. (2012). *Vibrations and Waves in Physics*. Cambridge University Press.

6. Hibbler, R. C. (2015). *Statics and Dynamics* (14th ed.).

7. Singh, R. N. (~2010s). *Waves and Oscillations* (2nd ed.).  
   (Dedicated chapters on SHM, damping, and forced vibrations.)

8. Feynman, R. P., Leighton, R. B., & Sands, M. (1963). *The Feynman Lectures on Physics, Vol. 1*. Addison-Wesley.  
   (Chapters 21–23: Harmonic motion, damping, and resonance.)
