<p><u><strong>Procedure for the experiment is as follows:</strong></u></p>

<strong><p style="color:#2867B2;">1. To study Simple Harmonic Motion</p></strong>
<ul>
<li><p>Step 1: Set the damping factor (b) = 0.</p></li>
<li><p>Step 2: Set v<sub>0</sub> = 0 m/s.</p></li>
<li><p>Step 3: Drag the ball to the maximum starting position, say x<sub>0</sub> = 10.0 m, and click the play animation button.</p></li>
<li><p>Step 4: Observe the position graph as shown in Fig. 1 and wait until the animation ends.</p></li>
<li><p>Step 5: To get the position and velocity graph as shown in Fig. 2, tick the velocity checkbox (V).</p></li>
</ul>
